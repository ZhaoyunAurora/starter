How to Play

Basic Controls

Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies.

Level Up System

Defeat monsters to gain experience and level up. Each level up allows you to choose a new ability or upgrade existing ones.

Power-ups

Collect gems from defeated enemies to power up your abilities and unlock new combinations.
